Academic Curriculum Vitae

Anthony is an urban and regional planner with advanced geospatial statistics expertise, and a former Senior MADIP Data Specialist with the Australian Bureau of Statistics where they trained and supervise data scientists within the Big Data Pathways section. They are excited by the promise of smart cities and a socially sustainable global society starting from the neighbourhood-scale. Their overarching career objective is clearing the open and big data expertise barrier so that urban researchers and the public can co-design liveable cities where socially diverse communities can thrive.
