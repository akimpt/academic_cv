Complete Academic Curriculum Vitae

Anthony is a sociologist with advanced geospatial statistics training that is excited by the promise of smart cities and a socially sustainable global society starting from the neighbourhood-scale. Their overarching career objective is clearing the open and big data expertise barrier so that urban researchers and the public can co-design liveable cities where socially diverse communities can thrive.
